Razorpay & COD payments
Razorpay is fully integrated for online payments with real-time verification. Cash on Delivery is also supported. Automatic invoice generation and order tracking built in.
Payment verified, order auto-confirmed
Coupons & Combo Offers
Create percentage or flat-rate discount coupons, set minimum cart values, usage limits, and expiry dates. Stack combo offers across seller products — all with a clean customer-facing UI.
Per-seller and platform-wide discounts
Walk-in POS system
A full point-of-sale terminal for offline / walk-in customers. Sellers can bill customers directly, apply coupons, and generate professional PDF receipts — shareable via signed links.
Signed shareable receipt links
Order & Returns management
Full order lifecycle — pending, accepted, packed, shipped, delivered, returned. Sellers manage their own order items. Customers request returns. Stock auto-adjusts at each stage.
7-stage order lifecycle
Mobile-first responsive UI
Every page — storefront, checkout, seller dashboard, POS — is fully responsive and optimised for mobile. Touch swipe on product images, drawer cart, native share API support.
Swipe gestures + native share
Ratings & Wishlist
Customers can rate products only after verified purchase. Wishlist system with cross-session persistence. Product share tracking across WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, and copy-link.
Verified purchase ratings only
QR & Product variants
Each seller product gets a unique QR code (UUID). Full product variant system — size, colour, weight — with combination keys. Per-variant pricing, stock, expiry and batch tracking.
UUID-keyed QR per SKU
Seller sub-stores

The Process

How it works for your business

01
You choose a plan
Pick the model that fits your situation — monthly hosting for a managed service, one-time license for full ownership, or revenue share for zero upfront cost.
02
We configure & brand it
We set up the platform with your brand name, logo, colours, domain, and payment gateway keys. Your marketplace goes live on your own domain — fully yours.
03
Onboard your sellers
Sellers sign up, create their sub-stores, add products and inventory. You approve them through the staff dashboard. All seller management is self-service.
04
Sell & scale
Customers shop, pay, rate, return. You collect commissions. We handle maintenance and updates. As your business grows, upgrade your plan or switch models any time.
