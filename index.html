Real Problems. Direct Solutions.
Do you face these struggles?
Most shop owners in Tamil Nadu deal with the same issues every day. Mayavisai solves all of them — cleanly and completely.
The Problem
Orders Getting Lost in WhatsApp

From morning to night, WhatsApp messages pile up. Which order is confirmed? Which is pending? Which was delivered? The confusion costs you real customers — and a missed customer rarely comes back.

Mayavisai Fix
Every Order Visible on Your Dashboard

When a customer orders from your online store, it appears instantly on your dashboard. Pending → Packed → Shipped → Delivered — 7 clear stages, full tracking. Nothing falls through the cracks.

The Problem
Handwritten Bills Erode Customer Trust

Walk-in customers ask for a bill. Handwritten receipts have errors, look unprofessional, and get lost. Without a clear record, returns become arguments. Customers lose confidence in your shop.

Mayavisai Fix
PDF Bill in 30 Seconds — via WhatsApp

Select products on your phone, apply a discount, tap Bill — a professional PDF with your shop name, items, total is ready in 30 seconds. Send it to the customer's WhatsApp instantly. No more arguments on returns.

The Problem
You Don't Know What's Left in Stock

You have to manually write down every sale. Combining online orders and walk-in sales is a mess. Overselling means disappointing customers and issuing refunds. You only find out when something runs out.

Mayavisai Fix
Stock Auto-Deducts on Every Sale

Every sale — online or in-store — instantly reduces stock. You get alerts when items run low. Out-of-stock items automatically hide on your online store. Overselling becomes impossible.

The Problem
You're Guessing What Sells Well

Without data, you don't know which products move fast and which sit unsold. You restock the wrong things and waste money. Giving records to your accountant becomes a monthly headache.

Mayavisai Fix
Full Sales Report — Download Any Month

Every product's sales count, total revenue, top sellers — all in a clean PDF. Easy for you, easy for your accountant. Make smart restocking decisions with real data, not guesswork.
